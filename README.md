# Mirai

### Explore. Find. Stream Seamlessly.

---

A sleek, lightweight anime hub engineered specifically for Android. Built to deliver a fluid, high-performance experience without intrusive clutter or unnecessary interruptions. Navigate your favorite series, track updates, and enjoy a high-quality streaming UI optimized for modern mobile hardware.

</div>

## ✨ Key Attributes

### 🔍 Search & Exploration
*   **Intuitive Browsing:** Instantly look through ongoing, highly-rated, and newly updated series.
*   **Quick Search Functionality:** Locate specific titles, movies, or franchises in seconds.
*   **Comprehensive Data:** Access deep metadata including episode breakdowns, user ratings, genres, and synopsis details.

### ⚡ Performance & Fluidity
*   **Optimized Architecture:** Designed to consume minimal system resources while maintaining high responsiveness across various hardware profiles.
*   **Native Feel:** Features buttery-smooth navigation paths and clean transitions built for standard Android systems.
*   **Seamless Delivery:** Built-in mechanisms to ensure you stay running on the latest build stability.

### 🎨 Visual Interface & Experience
*   **Zero Ad Interruptions:** Completely ad-free environment focusing purely on delivery, keeping your viewing entirely clean.
*   **Custom Themes:** Personalized visual layouts featuring integrated dark-mode configurations and aesthetic styles crafted intentionally to reduce eye strain.
*   **Tailored Layouts:** Keep track of your watch history and organize your customized bookmarks with ease.

### 📥 Offline Access
*   **External Media Downloading:** Save and store content directly to your device for smooth, uninterrupted playback anytime, anywhere.
> [!IMPORTANT]
> **iOS & iPadOS Download Notice:**
> Local video playback on iOS devices requires an external media player app (such as **VLC for Mobile**).

### ☁️ Cloud Sync & Account
*   **Cross-Device Progress Tracking:** Powered by a secure Firestore database backend to ensure your viewing history follows you anywhere.
*   **Account Authentication:** Access personalized cloud functionality safely through a mandatory user login gateway.
*   **Instant Manual Backups:** On-demand save features conveniently located directly within the anime information and watchlist dashboards to keep your data current.
